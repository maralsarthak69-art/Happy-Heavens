1. How to log in

  1. 1 Go to your website address and add /login/ at the end — for example: happy-heavens.onrender.com/login/
  2. 2 Enter your username and password, then click Log In.
  3. 3 After logging in, go to /dashboard/ — this is your home base for managing everything.
💡 Tip: Bookmark the dashboard page so you can open it directly next time.

2. Understanding your dashboard

When you open the dashboard, you'll see:

  • 📊 Stat cards at the top — today's orders, how many are pending, how many are confirmed, your total revenue from delivered orders, and today's custom requests.
  • ⚠️ Low stock warning — if any product has 3 or fewer units left, it shows up here in orange. Click it to go restock.
  • 🛍️ Recent orders — the last 20 orders. Each one shows the customer's name, phone, what they ordered, and the total. You can update the status and add notes right here.
  • 💡 Custom requests — the last 10 custom requests from customers. Shows their name, phone, and what they want.
  • ⚡ Quick action buttons at the bottom — shortcuts to add a product, edit products, manage stock, download orders, and more.

3. How to process an order step by step

When a customer places an order, here's exactly what to do:

  1. 1
    Check the payment. If the customer paid by QR, you'll see a "View proof ↗" link next to the order. Click it to see their payment screenshot. Verify the amount matches the order total. If they chose Cash on Delivery, you'll collect payment when you deliver.
  2. 2
    Confirm the order. Once you're happy with the payment (or it's COD), change the status to ✅ Confirmed and click Save. The customer will automatically get a WhatsApp message and email saying their order is confirmed.
  3. 3
    Prepare the order. Make the product. You can write a note to yourself in the Private Notes box — for example: "Preparing, will deliver Thursday". Customers never see this.
  4. 4
    When you're on the way to deliver, change the status to 🚚 Out for Delivery. The customer gets notified automatically.
  5. 5
    After delivery, change the status to 🎉 Delivered. Done! The order is complete.
✅ Remember: Every time you change the status, the customer automatically gets a WhatsApp message and email. You don't need to contact them manually.

4. What each order status means

⏳ Pending

A new order just came in. You haven't done anything yet. Check the payment and then confirm or reject it.

✅ Confirmed

You've accepted the order and verified the payment. Now prepare the product.

🚚 Out for Delivery

The product is on its way to the customer. Set this when you leave to deliver.

🎉 Delivered

The customer has received their order. Set this after successful delivery. Order is complete.

❌ Rejected

The payment failed, was fake, or you couldn't fulfil the order. Use this to cancel an order. The customer will be notified.

5. How to handle a custom request

When a customer fills in the "Customize Your Ideas" form on the website, you get a notification email. Here's what to do:

  1. 1 Open your dashboard. You'll see the request in the Custom Requests section on the right side.
  2. 2 Read what the customer wants. If they uploaded a reference photo, click it to see it full size.
  3. 3 Call or WhatsApp the customer on the phone number shown. Tap the green phone number — it opens WhatsApp directly.
  4. 4 Discuss the details, agree on a price, and arrange payment and delivery directly with them.
💡 Custom requests don't go through the normal order system — they're handled personally between you and the customer.

6. How to add a new product

  1. 1 From the dashboard, click "➕ Add New Product". This opens the product form.
  2. 2 Fill in the Product Name. The URL Name below it fills itself automatically — don't change it.
  3. 3 Choose the Category (e.g. Bouquets, Hampers).
  4. 4 Write a Description — what it's made of, what occasion it's for, size, etc.
  5. 5 Set the Price in rupees and the Stock (how many you have ready).
  6. 6 Scroll down to 📷 Product Photos. Click Choose File to upload photos. You can add multiple photos — the first one is the main photo customers see.
  7. 7 Make sure "Visible on website" is ticked, then click Save at the bottom.
📸 Photo tip: Use clear, well-lit photos with a clean background. The better the photo, the more customers will buy.

7. How to edit a product

  1. 1 From the dashboard, click "✏️ Edit Products".
  2. 2 Find the product you want to change and click on it to expand the edit form.
  3. 3 Change the name, price, stock, description, or category as needed.
  4. 4 To hide a product without deleting it, untick "Show on website". Customers won't see it but it stays in your system.
  5. 5 Click Save Changes.
📷 To add or change photos, click "Open full edit (for photos) ↗" at the bottom of the edit form. This opens the full admin where you can manage photos.

8. How to restock a product

Stock goes down automatically when customers order. When you make more products, update the stock so customers can order again.

  1. 1 From the dashboard, click "📦 Manage Stock".
  2. 2 Find the product. Products with 0 stock show in red, low stock (1–3) in orange.
  3. 3 Type the new number in the box (e.g. if you made 5 more, type 5) and click Save.
⚠️ Important: If a product shows "Out of stock", customers cannot add it to their cart. Restock it as soon as you have more ready.

9. How to download your orders as a spreadsheet

You can download all your orders as a file that opens in Excel or Google Sheets.

  1. 1 From the dashboard, click "📥 Download Orders (CSV)".
  2. 2 A file will download to your phone or computer. Open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.
  3. 3 The file contains: order number, date, customer name, phone, address, items ordered, total, payment method, status, and your notes.
💡 You can also download only pending orders by going to: /dashboard/export/orders.csv?status=PENDING

10. Useful tips

  • 📱 Every customer phone number on the dashboard is a tap-to-WhatsApp link. Tap it to open a WhatsApp chat with that customer instantly.
  • 🔔 You get an email notification every time a new order is placed or a custom request is submitted. Check your email regularly.
  • 📝 Use the Private Notes box on each order to keep track of what you've done — e.g. "Spoke to customer, delivering Saturday". Only you can see these notes.
  • 👁️ If you want to temporarily stop selling a product (e.g. you're on holiday), untick "Show on website" in the product editor. Tick it again when you're back.
  • 🔐 The dashboard is only visible to you when you're logged in. Customers cannot see it.
  • ❓ If something looks wrong or broken on the website, contact your developer. Don't try to fix it yourself — you might accidentally break something.
